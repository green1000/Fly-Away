DO'S AND DON'TS
When going on a Holiday!

Some things you should be prepared about when going on a vacation is don't pack everything in your suitcase, don't let jetlag get the best of you,
don't spend too much time on your phone or in your room, do experience the culture of your destination and don't explore the city by yourself.
Another thing you should should do is respecting the culture and the people of this place and don't litter.
Don't do anything offensive and you will be good to go!!
